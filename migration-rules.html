Rules of migration control

Foreign citizens of countries with which Russia has restored air communication on a reciprocal basis can return to Russia for full-time study: Egypt, India, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan.

A foreign citizen is obliged:

  1. Notify the staff of the Department for work with Foreign Students and International Relations by e-mail at least 10 days in advance foreign-students@mail.ru about the date of entry into the Russian Federation and the border crossing point;
  2. Receive a written message with permission to enter the territory of the Russian Federation from employees of the department for work with foreign students and international relations;
  3. Not earlier than 3 calendar days before arrival in Russia, pass a PCR test for COVID-19 in your country;
  4. When crossing the border of the Russian Federation and settling into a university dormitory, have a medical certificate in Russian or English with a negative test result for coronavirus infection.

After arriving in Russia:

  1. Within 72 hours after entering the territory of Russia, re-test for COVID-19 by PCR;
  2. Maintain self-isolation at the place of stay until a negative test result is obtained;
  3. During the period of isolation, attend all training sessions online;
  4. After receiving the result of the second PCR test, provide copies of both certificates with a negative PCR result to the department for work with foreign students and international relations;
  5. If there are two negative test results, get admission to study sessions in a group according to the organized format of training at the university;
  6. In case of any deterioration in health, immediately seek medical help by calling 103 or 112, self-isolate and inform the staff of the department for working with foreign students and international relations at the available phone numbers.

FOR ANY QUESTIONS, PLEASE CONTACT THE STAFF OF THE DEPARTMENT FOR WORKING WITH FOREIGN STUDENTS AND INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS:

Phone: (3952) 24-35-09, e-mail: foreign-students@mail.ru or in the WhatsApp groups available to you
